Below are the areas where we most often add value. But the right engagement is the one we build together — purpose-built for companies in the early stages of commercial growth, and focused on what matters most at your stage.

01
Fractional CFO Services

"CFO-caliber support, built around your needs and budget."

Senior finance leadership that brings clarity to your numbers and takes ownership of the finance agenda — so you and your team can stay focused on running and growing the business. Designed for companies that need CFO-caliber oversight without the full-time commitment, every engagement is scoped and structured around where you are today and where you're headed.

Some examples include

  • Financial planning and analysis
  • Budgeting and forecasting
  • Cash flow management
  • KPI and dashboard design
02
Business Growth Advisory

"Bringing financial rigor to the way you grow."

Early-stage commercial growth demands more than a sales strategy — it needs a financial architecture to support it. Working alongside commercial leadership to design the selling models, incentive structures, and performance dashboards that drive the right behaviors and make growth sustainable.

Some examples include

  • Commercial sales model assessment
  • Sales incentive plan design and infrastructure
  • Sales performance and growth driver dashboards
  • Revenue forecasting and pipeline analytics
03
Market Entry Services

"Enter with confidence. Operate with clarity."

For foreign-owned businesses — particularly those from Australia — establishing their presence in the US and Canadian markets, we provide the financial expertise to build it right and the ongoing fractional finance leadership to keep it running. From initial setup through to steady-state North American operations.

Some examples include

  • Market entry strategy and financial planning
  • Finance infrastructure and entity setup
  • US and Canadian tax and compliance readiness
  • Ongoing fractional finance leadership for North America

Meet the Founder
Big Four discipline. Commercial instinct. Trusted partner for the journey.
Joe Buttacavoli CPA (inactive) CGMA
Founder & Principal Consultant

Joe Buttacavoli is a Strategic Finance Executive who has dedicated the last two decades to serving growth-stage companies across the medical technology and life science sectors. He founded Right On Insights with a clear purpose — to channel a unique blend of finance, commercial, and operational experience into the growth-stage companies navigating the hardest parts of early commercial growth.

His career spans public accounting, corporate finance, and general management — from his early years at a Big Four firm serving life sciences clients across the US and Australia, to senior finance roles at publicly traded med-tech companies, to serving as a founding member of the North America leadership team of a global medical technology company, scaling the business from startup to over $100M in annual revenue.

That experience is the foundation — and putting it to work for growth-stage companies at the moments that matter most is the mission. Every engagement is built on the belief that the right insight, at the right stage, changes outcomes.
